About Me

Hi, my name is Cliff Jiang. I am a junior at the University of California, Santa Cruz, studying game design.

Design Focus

I design interactive systems that shape how players behave and understand a game. In my projects, I focus on how players learn rules through interaction, whether by drawing paths, repeating time loops, or interpreting unclear social cues.

System-Based Interaction

I am interested in projects where simple rules can lead to complex player behavior over time. In one project, players construct their own routes instead of following predefined paths. In another, progression depends on what the player remembers across multiple loops rather than what they collect.

Player Discovery

I am particularly interested in systems where players are not just following instructions, but actively discovering and shaping the structure of the game itself.
